---
term: llms.txt
title: llms.txt
definition: A site-root text file (analogous to robots.txt and sitemap.xml) that lists structured links to a site's content for AI consumers.
category: standards
relatedTerms:
  - AEO
  - sitemap
---

**`llms.txt`** is an emerging convention proposed by [Jeremy Howard](https://llmstxt.org). It lives at `/llms.txt` and gives AI agents a hand-curated index of your site, optionally with markdown summaries.

Dualmark generates `/llms.txt` automatically from the same config that drives your collections.
